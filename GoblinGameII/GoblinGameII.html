Tutorial

Defeat the Goblin to win. Whoever's HP is reduced down to 0 loses.
Hit [Next Turn] to start the game.
On your turn select an action. Example: [Attack]
When your turn is over press [Next Turn].

Knight Guide

[Attack] - Deal Damage if Player's ATK > Enemy's DEF
[Magic] - Lose 1HP to cast a spell that deals 0-MAG - Enemy's RES
[Charge] - Increase ATK by 40% for one Turn
[Defend] - Increase DEF by 40% for one Turn
Allows you to hold [Charge] for one more Turn
[Heal] - Use 1 ITM to heal for HP/2-MAG
[Give Up] - Forfeits the Fight

Princess Guide

[Attack] - Deal Damage if Player's ATK > Enemy's DEF
[Counter] - Lose 2HP to Counter Attack if the Enemy Attacks the Next Turn
[Gambit] - Sacrifice DEF by 50% to Boost ATK by 50%
[Defend] - Increase DEF by 40% for one Turn
Cannot be Selected When Using [Gambit]
[Heal] - Use 1 ITM to heal for HP/2-MAG
[Give Up] - Forfeits the Fight

Mage Guide

[Attack] - Deal Damage if Player's ATK > Enemy's DEF
[Magic] - Lose 1HP to Cast a Spell that Deals 0-MAG - Enemy's RES
[Magic Charge] - Increase MAG by 40% for one Turn
[Debuff] - Decrease all Enemy stats by 40%
[Siphon] - Use 1 ITM to Heal x2 the Damage Dealt
[Give Up] - Forfeits the Fight

Huntress Guide

[Attack] - Deal Damage if Player's ATK > Enemy's DEF
[Poison] - Lose 1HP to Poison Enemy for Several Turns but has Chance to Poison Self
[Piercing Arrow] - Deal Half Damage While Ignoring Enemy DEF and 100% Accurate
[Evade] - Decrease Enemy SKL and CRT by 80%
[Heal] - Use 1 ITM to Heal for HP/2-MAG
[Give Up] - Forfeits the Fight

Ninja Guide

[Attack] - Deal Damage if Player's ATK > Enemy's DEF
[Strike] - Lose 1HP to Deal Damage Based off Enemy Buffs - Cannot Kill
[Steal] - Steal Enemy ITM
[Vanish] - Decrease Enemy SKL and CRT to 0
[Heal] - Use 1 ITM to heal for HP/2-MAG
[Give Up] - Forfeits the fight
